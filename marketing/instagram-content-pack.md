Your doctor checks your LDL. That's fine.

→ LDL measures cholesterol CONTENT

Optimal ApoB: < 90 mg/dL
Ideal (high risk): < 70 mg/dL

If you've never tested yours, ask for it on your next panel. It's the single best predictor of heart disease risk — better than LDL, total cholesterol, or any ratio.

If you're over 40, you should probably be taking CoQ10.

Critical if you take berberine or statins — they deplete CoQ10 through the mevalonate pathway.
